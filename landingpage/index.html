Welcome to QCU Land

Heed the call, adventurer! A new quest awaits thee in the digital realm of QCU Land. Hone your skills, for in this world, knowledge is your sharpest blade and vigilance your strongest shield.

The Chronicle of QCU Land

In the hallowed halls of QCU Land, a shadow looms. As a new student, you must embark on a perilous journey through a world fraught with digital dangers. From corrupted scrolls (phishing emails) to treacherous portals (unsecured Wi-Fi), your quest is to safeguard the kingdom's secrets and your own digital soul.

The Armory: Your Arsenal

Explore a vast and immersive 3D world, rendered in a classic, low-poly art style.

Forge your own path in a story-driven adventure with branching narratives.

Face real-world cybersecurity threats, disguised as mythical beasts and magical traps.

Master the intuitive point-and-click interface to navigate the world and vanquish your foes.

Level up your cybersecurity skills and watch your reputation grow with every victory.

The Library: Ancient Knowledge

The wise mages of QCU Land have collected ancient tomes of knowledge to aid you in your quest. Study them well, and you will learn to:

Identify the tell-tale signs of dark magic (phishing, malware, and social engineering).

Think critically about your online actions and protect your digital identity.

Respond to digital threats with the wisdom and courage of a seasoned adventurer.

Develop the habits of a true cybersecurity champion.

The Battlefield: The Path to Glory

Your journey through QCU Land will be fraught with challenges. To succeed, you must:

Explore: Traverse the sprawling 3D campus of QCU Land.

Interact: Converse with the inhabitants of the land, inspect ancient artifacts, and decipher cryptic messages.

Challenge: Test your skills in scenario-based puzzles and timed events.

Outcome: Your choices will determine your fate. Earn reputation and unlock new abilities, or fall prey to the digital darkness.

Progress: Complete quests to unlock new regions and unravel the mysteries of QCU Land.

The Forge: The Making of a Legend

QCU Land was forged in the fires of modern technology:

Game Engine: three.js, for a lightweight and powerful 3D experience.

Frontend Framework: React, for a dynamic and responsive user interface.

State Management: Zustand, for efficient and predictable state management.

Styling: CSS, for a visually stunning and immersive world.
